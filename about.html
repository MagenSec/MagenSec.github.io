About MagenSec

We built MagenSec because we believe every organization deserves to know — and prove — their security posture.

Our enemy isn't other products. It's the unknown security state that most organizations live in. Antivirus alone isn’t enough if you cannot continuously prove it is working.

MagenSec bridges the gap between protection and proof, eliminating the unknown security state entirely through continuous endpoint intelligence and MAGI, our decision engine.

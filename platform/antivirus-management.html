What MagenSec manages

We help you keep built-in protection healthy, updated, and properly configured - without pretending antivirus alone solves security.

Protection health

Detect when real-time protection, tamper protection, or cloud-delivered protection is disabled.

Signature currency

Track outdated signatures and alert when endpoints fall behind your expected update window.

Scheduled scans

Verify that periodic scans are configured and that scan execution remains healthy over time.

Policy drift

Flag unexpected exclusions, disabled settings, or weakened Defender policies that reduce protection.

Fleet visibility

See which devices need action now instead of checking Defender status device-by-device.

Audit evidence

Show that antivirus was active, updated, and configured correctly when audit or incident questions arise.
